Mobile Racing Championship

Race with just your CellPhone and the Real Racing 3 Game, without needing an Advanced Racing Simulator.

Who we are?

We are fans of F1 and motorsport above all else. With the passion for f1, we decided to create this championship to serve the mobile racing fan audience, since most competitions are aimed at console games or advanced simulators.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make Formula RR competitive, attractive, fun and give everyone the opportunity to participate. All you need is a Cell Phone and the Real Racing 3 game installed on it. And it is also part of our mission to contribute to the growth and engagement of the F1 community.
